Welcome to our Prop & Tee Pty Ltd Surveying App

This is a Surveying App!

This app can use to quickly size up a property and determine how much it would cost to construct.

This project contain features that can determine the acreage, perimeter, and approximate quantity of fence required to surround the property.

If someone uses our app to walk along a property's or area's borders, it will show them the property's area and perimeter as well as an estimation of the amount of fence that would be needed to surround.
